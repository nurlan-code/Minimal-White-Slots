What is Responsible Gaming?

Responsible gaming means enjoying casino games in a way that is safe, controlled, and doesn't negatively impact your life, relationships, or finances. Even though our platform uses only virtual credits, understanding responsible gaming principles helps create healthy gaming habits.

Set Time Limits

Decide how much time you want to spend playing and stick to it. Take regular breaks to maintain a healthy balance.

Play for Fun

Remember that gaming should be enjoyable entertainment, not a way to make money or escape from problems.

Maintain Relationships

Don't let gaming interfere with your relationships, work, or other important activities in your life.

Stay Balanced

Keep gaming as one of many activities in your life, not the primary focus of your time and attention.

Warning Signs of Problem Gaming

Even with virtual credits, it's important to recognize signs that gaming might be becoming problematic:

Behavioral Signs

  • Spending excessive time gaming
  • Neglecting responsibilities or relationships
  • Feeling anxious or irritable when not gaming
  • Lying about time spent gaming
  • Unable to stop or control gaming sessions

Emotional Signs

  • Using gaming to escape from problems or negative emotions
  • Feeling guilty or ashamed about gaming habits
  • Mood swings related to gaming outcomes
  • Loss of interest in other activities
  • Depression or anxiety when unable to game

Self-Help Tools

Here are some tools and strategies to help you maintain healthy gaming habits:

Gaming Schedule

Create a specific schedule for gaming sessions and stick to it. This helps maintain control and balance.

Session Timers

Set timers for your gaming sessions to ensure you don't lose track of time.

Activity Checklist

Complete important tasks before gaming to ensure gaming doesn't interfere with responsibilities.

Accountability Partner

Share your gaming goals with a friend or family member who can help you stay accountable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it possible to get addicted to demo games?

While demo games don't involve real money, excessive gaming can still lead to behavioral addiction. It's important to maintain healthy gaming habits even with virtual credits.

How can I help someone with a gaming problem?

Be supportive and non-judgmental. Encourage them to seek professional help and provide information about available resources. Don't enable their problematic behavior.

What should I do if I think I have a problem?

Reach out for help immediately. Contact the National Problem Gambling Helpline, speak with a counselor, or join a support group. Early intervention is key to recovery.

Are there age restrictions for gaming?

Yes, our platform is intended for users 18 and older. Parents should monitor their children's gaming activities and educate them about responsible gaming.
